What is a copypaste?

Copypasta - the latest modern weapons of mass destruction, which works on the principle of copying text and inserting it. And it does not matter where the text comes from - it is important where it is inserted!

How it works

  • 3. Copy the paste (ctrl+c) and insert it (ctrl+v);

Where to use copypastas?

The purpose of writing pasta is to provoke a reaction from the opponent. They can be either a separate commentator in discussions, or a streamer or chat in general. If this is a chat, then a good reaction will be the further spread of pasta in the chat, that is, its active SPAM!

If the goal is a commentator, choose the most realistic paste, such that the opponent does not guess that he is being TROLLED.

If the goal is a streamer, we act similarly to the previous case. As a result, the following actions can be obtained. Thw Stramer will...
